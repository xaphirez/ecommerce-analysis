# UK E-Commerce Sales Analysis 🛒

## Overview
End-to-end data analysis of a UK online retailer's transactions 
from 2010-2011, covering 500,000+ rows of real transaction data.

## Business Questions Answered
- What is the total revenue?
- Which country generates the most revenue?
- What are the top 10 best selling products?
- What is the monthly revenue trend?
- What is the cancellation rate?

## Key Findings
- Total Revenue: £10,642,110.80
- Total Orders: 19,960
- Total Customers: 4,338
- UK dominates with 84.6% of total revenue
- Revenue peaks in November (Christmas gift season)
- 19.22% cancellation rate with suspicious patterns found
